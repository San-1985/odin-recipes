Zucchini soup recipe

Description

Ingredients

  • 50g onions
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 700g Zucchini, in pieces
  • 500g water
  • powder soup for 1/2 l of soup
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • pepper
  • 10g butter
  • 40g cheese spread

Steps

  1. Put onions and garlic into the Thermomix and chop.
  2. Add butter and saut√©.
  3. Add zucchini and shred.
  4. Add water, soup powder, salt and pepper and cook for 15 minutes
  5. Add cheese spread and mix for 30 seconds.
