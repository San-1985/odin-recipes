Spaghetti aglio e olio

Description

A quick and easy recipe that tastes delicious.

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 150g olive oil
  • salt
  • parsley

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti al dente in salted water.
  2. Peel and chop garlic, wash parsley and chop.
  3. Head olive oil with garlic for 3 minutes and add the cooked pasta.
  4. Mix everything and top with parsley.
