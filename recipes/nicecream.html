Banana Nicecream Recipe

Description

Banana ice cream or "nice cream" is my favorite healthy dessert because itâ€™s so healthy without any added sugar and it's quick and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 3 frozen bananas
  • a pinch of vanilla (powder or liquid)
  • 2 tablespoons cacao powder

Steps

  1. Add frozen bananas in chunks to your Vitamix or other food processor.
  2. Process bananas until they have a smooth and creamy ice-like texture.
  3. Add vanilla powder and cacao if desired and process for just another few seconds until everything is mixed evenly.
  4. Serve and enjoy!
